# Translation Style Guide

## Tone

- Keep language professional, clear, and operational.
- Avoid over-marketing language.
- Prefer short sentences for CTA and navigation labels.

## Consistency

- Reuse glossary terms exactly.
- Keep section headers semantically equivalent between languages.
- Keep metric and architecture names consistent across pages.

## Formatting

- Date format:
  - TH: `21 ธันวาคม 2023`
  - EN: `December 21, 2023`
- Number format should follow locale conventions.
- Keep legal identifiers and registration numbers unchanged.

## Technical tokens

- Do not translate: API, CLI, GitHub, URLs, email addresses.
- Product and company names stay in canonical form.

## Line length and UI

- Navigation label target: <= 18 characters when possible.
- Button text target: <= 24 characters.
- Avoid long unbreakable strings.

## Legal pages

- Preserve full meaning and legal scope.
- No shortening or summarization in translated legal content.
